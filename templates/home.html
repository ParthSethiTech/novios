Why Novios?

Click below on the tabs to read more

Novios has many great audio editing features built-in, such as displaying waveforms on the timeline, or even rendering the waveform as part of your video. You can also split the audio from your video clip, and adjust each audio channel individually.
Novios consists of normal to very advanced effects that could be applied to various things in only a few clicks
Plugins are made after testing them properly and are specially made for advanced designing
Everything is labelled properly so that users could refer to them and use a feature accordingly
Most video editors take minutes and sometimes even hours to render but novios reders in a few seconds only using multi-layer movie generation with lazy loading, so it loads as you watch.
It is available for all operating existing softwares. So share it to everyone!
Software includes tools to convert file formats, cut segments (trimming), join clips, re-sequence clips, adjust brightness/contrast, rotate or crop footage, add sound or add transitions
Our advanced video editing timeline has a ton of great features for helping you build a great video project. Dragging and dropping, resizing clips, zooming in and out, alignment, preset animations and settings, slicing, snapping, and more! Just drag a file onto the timeline to begin!
When arranging clips in a video project, images on the higher tracks/layers will be displayed on top, and the lower tracks will be displayed behind them. Much like a stack of paper, items on top cover up items below them. And if you cut any holes out (i.e. transparency) the lower images will show though.
Tracks are used to layer images, videos, and audio in a project. You can create as many layers as needed, such as watermarks, background audio tracks, background videos... Any transparency will show through the layer below it. Tracks can also be moved up, down, or locked.

About Novios

Novios is a free video editing software which is designed by two students from Delhi Public School Noida- Parth Sethi and Laksh Saini. An idea of a free and easy-to-use video editor was inspired by the struggles of our motion graphics editor. We hope you like the app as well as the website interface.
